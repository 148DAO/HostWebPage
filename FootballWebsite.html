The NFL or the National Football Leauge is a league if 32 teams that compete for the Championship. It is divided by the AFC (American Football Conference) and the NFC (National Football Conference). The NFL is actually not National for it only consists of American teams in different famous American citys or towns. It is one of the most famous sports in America if not the most popular sports in America. Many people not just in America but worldwide come together becoming United to watch the epic Championship game each year!

Like I said before the NFL is divided in to two conferences, the American Football Conference or the AFC, and the National Football Conference or the NFC.
